Our Beauty Legacy

Where artistry meets elegance in every piece we create

Crafting Beauty Since 2010

Founded with a passion for exquisite craftsmanship, Auranest began as a small boutique in Mumbai with just five jewelry designs and a dream. Today, we've grown into an internationally recognized brand while maintaining our commitment to handcrafted quality.

Our founder, Priya Sharma, started by creating custom pieces for friends and family. The overwhelming response led to our first store, and now we're proud to serve customers worldwide with our unique blend of traditional techniques and contemporary design.

Our Core Values

Ethical Sourcing

We use only conflict-free diamonds and responsibly mined gemstones, ensuring our jewelry is as beautiful ethically as it is aesthetically.

Artisan Craftsmanship

Each piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans using techniques passed down through generations of jewelry makers.

Personalized Service

From custom designs to beauty consultations, we provide tailored experiences for each client's unique needs.
